So... Why NewerGrounds?

The name NewerGrounds stems from the original website, NewGrounds. Our website is intended to function similarly, but NewGrounds has some more neat features. They have games, unlimited games,
AND bacon as well. Also, Newgrounds has built-in blog features that the developer, Tom Fulp, uses to update users and highlight special people. Newgrounds also has calendar-events for game jams, art competitions, and more!
Also, small unknown fact: While NewGrounds is typically blacklisted on search engines (for both reasonable and unreasonable reasons), most content on the internet originated there. Early internet culture like Homestuck, Ultimate Showdown, and even the earliest YouTube animations all appeared on NewGrounds first!
